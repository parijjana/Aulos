# Feature: Podcast Discovery & Management

## Summary
A comprehensive podcasting suite including discovery via Apple Podcasts/PodcastIndex API, RSS feed parsing, and background downloading.

## Rationale
Enables users to manage their spoken-word subscriptions alongside their music library in a single interface.

## Expected Behavior
- **Discovery**: Search for podcasts and view trending shows.
- **Episodes**: List and play episodes directly from RSS.
- **Downloading**: Save episodes locally with configurable retention (Keep X episodes).
- **Favorites**: Pinned episodes are moved to a `favorites/` folder to prevent auto-deletion.
- **Notes**: Display show notes with clickable timestamps for seeking.
